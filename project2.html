Aletheia - Sustainable Sailboat

A competitive sailboat designed for the R3 Sailing Class, focusing on eco-friendly materials and high-performance engineering. Won the 2023 Eco Marine Challenge with innovative bio-composite solutions.

Key Innovations

  • Flax fiber reinforced composite hull
  • 3D printed bio-polymer components
  • Solar-assisted navigation system
  • Recyclable structural components

Achievements

  • 40% lighter than traditional designs
  • 30% faster hull penetration
  • First place in Eco Design category
